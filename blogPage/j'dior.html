j'dior

Being sick sucks. Being sick in Paris at Dior Exhibition? A BIG NO-NO. So I spent last month mostly in bed trying to survive and considering becoming a Voldemort, no nose, no-problem…

Paris in November is probably not the most pleasant place on the planet, but it is magical. And standing 90 min under the Notre-Dame waiting for the light show seems to be a quite good idea even if it’s around 5°C and you ate 7 hours before. Pure magical experience!

But let’s get back to the topic: Dior. I’m so happy that the exhibition was in Paris -where everything began. The House of Dior launched its first spring/summer collection in 1947 and the first Dior perfume -Miss Dior (btw named after Christian’s sister). The history of Dior is absolutely terrific, just check the article Vogue.uk about Christian Dior and his company.
