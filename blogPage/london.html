When in Rome, do as the Romans do

Ok, not Rome, but London and not Romans but Londoners. But you get my point.

When you’re in the city that is almost 2000 years old what do you do? You shop. And this is what I was doing. Because I’m on a budget I was jumping from Primark to Primark (in my heart forever- Primani- Armani for people on a budget). Because this is what people actually love doing in England -they love buying stuff- and this is one of the reasons why I feel British in my heart.

I love that people are not afraid of wearing clothes in London. And guess what? This sentence actually has sense. You might even say a sense of style. People love shoes, bags and labels. There was a girl wearing sweatpants and having no makeup, she was just buying some essentials in the shop around the corner. But what was on her back? Gucci backpack. And this is what I respect and admire. She spent lots of money on that backpack and she is actually using it!

Another thing that I think is fantastic? Men’s fashion. Guys are doing some amazing job there! Jackets, shirts, trousers, shoes, socks: everything fits perfectly. I think that I can even risk and make a statement that in London there are more well-dressed men than woman. And I totally get it! It’s not that easy to be outstanding in that huge city! But what is absolutely fabulous? London gives you all possible sources, places and people so you can be whoever you want to be and whatever you want to be -because it will accept just the way you are.

So if anyone would ever ask me: – Tea with a dash of milk?- I’ll answer without a second of hesitation:

– Darling, always!
